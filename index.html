La Belle is one-of-a-kind flagship brought to you by WOW IEDC at MESCE which precisely fosters women empowerment and entrepreneurship among students and beyond.

La Belle involves workshops, talk shows, panel discussions, cultural events, entertainment shows, competitions, games and much more spanned across two vibrant days. With La Belle, we aim to bridge the gap between the women community in technology and entrepreneurship and to build a space for these individuals to learn and grow . Furthermore, we look forward to promoting and fostering small businesses and startup ideas among the student community.
